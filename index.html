About Me

Hi, I'm Mansour. This website is for my Computer Applications in Linguistics project. It shows how technology can be used to teach and practice English language skills.

What is this course?

This course connects language with technology. It introduces tools used in language learning, corpus linguistics, computer-assisted language learning, and basic text processing.

Lessons

Lesson 1: Tokenization

Tokenization means breaking a text into smaller parts, usually words or tokens.

Lesson 2: Lemmatization

Lemmatization reduces words to their base form. For example: is, are, am → be.

Lesson 3: Corpus Linguistics

A corpus is a collection of electronic texts used to study real language use.
